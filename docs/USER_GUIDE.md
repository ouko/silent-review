# User Guide

Silent Review is a mobile-first app where you create 5-second silent video reviews and guess other people's ratings.

## Getting Started

1. Open the app in your mobile browser.
2. Create an account or log in with email/OAuth.
3. Browse the "For You" feed.

## Creating a Review

1. Tap the record button.
2. Search for the product you're reviewing (or add a new one).
3. Hold the record button for 5 seconds, or upload a video from your gallery.
4. Set your real rating (1–10), add an optional caption and tag.
5. Tap **Post review**.

## Guessing

1. Watch a review.
2. Tap the number you think matches the creator's real rating.
3. Tap **Reveal** to see if you were right.
4. Earn points: 10 for exact, 5 for off-by-1, 2 for off-by-2.

## Streaks & Achievements

- Maintain a daily streak by creating or guessing every day.
- Unlock achievements like First Guess, Perfect 10, and Week Streak.
- Compete on the leaderboard.

## Sharing

After revealing a review, tap **Share** or **Copy link** to share it outside the app. You can also export a platform-optimized video.

## Profile

Visit your profile to see your reviews, stats, streak, and achievements. Follow other users to see their reviews in your Following feed.

## Tips

- Keep reviews under 5 seconds and well-lit.
- Use tags to help others discover your review.
- Guess honestly — the algorithm rewards engagement.
